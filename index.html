WHO ARE WE?

We are a team who believes that by keeping fit together and share our results, we can connect with each other during the pandemic. Therefore, we design SportConnect, an website which we can take part in fitness challenges and to share our results with the world. There are many different challenges to take, from fitness challenges like pushups to sport challenges like long-distance running.

MOST POPULAR CHALLENGES

Push-up Challenge

Try to beat your record everyday for a week, then share your results here!
